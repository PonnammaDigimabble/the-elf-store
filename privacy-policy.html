Privacy Notice

LAST UPDATED: February 16, 2026

This notice describes how the ELF Store handles your personal information. We believe in transparency and giving you control over your pet’s data.

1. Introduction

Your privacy is of paramount importance to the ELF Store. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Platform or use our services. We are committed to protecting the personal data of our users and their pets.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Pet Profiles: Pet name, species, breed, age, medical history, and vaccination records.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing information (processed via secure third-party providers).
  • Consultation Data: Symptoms described, photos uploaded for vet review, and session notes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate veterinary consultations and pet health management.
  • Process and deliver e-commerce orders.
  • Send appointment reminders and service updates.
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity.

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. This includes:

  • End-to-end encryption for video consultations.
  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmissions.
  • Secure, partitioned database storage.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Veterinary Professionals: To enable medical consultations and care.
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping companies, and cloud hosting partners.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • The right to rectify inaccurate information.
  • The right to request deletion of your account and data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@theelfstore.com.
